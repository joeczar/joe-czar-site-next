Self-care is not selfish, it's essential. Taking care of yourself is important for your mental health, and can help you feel happier, more relaxed, and more resilient. Here's why:

- It reduces stress and anxiety
- It helps you maintain healthy relationships
- It boosts your self-esteem
- It improves your overall well-being
- It allows you to better manage your emotions
