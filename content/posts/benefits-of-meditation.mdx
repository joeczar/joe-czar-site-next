Meditation is a practice that has been around for centuries, and for good reason. It has been shown to have numerous benefits for mental and physical health, including:

- Reducing stress and anxiety
- Improving focus and concentration
- Enhancing creativity
- Boosting immunity
- Increasing feelings of happiness and well-being
