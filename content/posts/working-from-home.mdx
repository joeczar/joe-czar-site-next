Working from home can be a great way to stay productive and avoid the distractions of the office. However, it can also be a challenge to stay focused and motivated when you're surrounded by the comforts of home. Here are 10 tips to help you stay productive while working from home:

1. Set up a dedicated workspace
2. Stick to a schedule
3. Take breaks
4. Get dressed
5. Avoid distractions
6. Use time-tracking tools
7. Stay connected with colleagues
8. Set boundaries with family and friends
9. Prioritize your tasks
10. Stay motivated
